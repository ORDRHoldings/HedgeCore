# Privacy Notice

**Last updated:** 2026-04-25 · **Version:** 1.0

This privacy notice explains how **ORDR TreasuryFX** ("ORDR", "we", "us") collects, uses, and shares personal information when you visit our website or use our services. It is written for the public — for the contractual data-processing terms that govern Personal Data we process on behalf of customers, see our [Data Processing Addendum](dpa.md).

---

## Scope

This notice applies to:

- Visitors to **ordrtreasuryfx.com** and related marketing pages
- Prospective customers who contact us, request demos, or download materials
- Users of the ORDR TreasuryFX application **in their personal capacity** (account creation, authentication, support)

It does **not** apply to:

- Customer Data that we process **on behalf of** our business customers as a Processor under a Data Processing Addendum — those terms govern that Processing
- Personal information collected by third-party websites linked from our marketing pages

## What we do **not** do

- We do **not** sell your personal information.
- We do **not** share your personal information with advertisers or data brokers.
- We do **not** use your data to train machine-learning models. (No ML in our product, no ML on our customer or visitor data.)
- We do **not** use cross-site tracking cookies (Facebook Pixel, Google Analytics, etc.).
- We do **not** require account creation to read our marketing or documentation pages.

## How we use personal information

We use personal information to:

1. Provide and operate the ORDR TreasuryFX service
2. Authenticate users and secure accounts
3. Respond to inquiries and provide customer support
4. Bill for services and process payments
5. Send service-related communications (security alerts, downtime notices, feature announcements relevant to use)
6. Send marketing communications **with your consent** (you can withdraw consent at any time)
7. Improve our website, product, and content based on aggregate analytics
8. Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security threats
9. Comply with legal obligations (e.g., tax records, anti-money-laundering due diligence, regulatory reporting)
10. Defend legal claims and exercise legal rights

## How we share personal information

We share personal information only as set forth below:

1. **With sub-processors** that provide infrastructure, observability, and operational services on our behalf, listed at [Sub-Processors](sub-processors.md). Sub-processors are bound by contractual data-protection obligations no less protective than those of this notice.
2. **With professional advisors** (lawyers, auditors, accountants) under confidentiality, where necessary for legitimate business purposes.
3. **In a corporate transaction** (merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets), where personal information may be transferred to the acquiring or surviving entity, subject to confidentiality obligations.
4. **As required by law** in response to subpoenas, court orders, or other legal process — but only after careful review of the request's validity and scope, and with notice to affected individuals where lawful.
5. **With your consent** for any other disclosure not described above.

## Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

- **Access** the personal information we hold about you

## California privacy rights (CCPA / CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the CCPA/CPRA:

- **Right to know** what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and (in our case, do not) sell or share
- **Right to delete** personal information we have collected about you
- **Right to correct** inaccurate personal information
- **Right to opt out** of the sale or sharing of personal information — **we do not sell or share personal information**, so this right is automatically honored
- **Right to limit use of sensitive personal information** — we do not collect sensitive personal information for purposes other than providing the service

Categories of personal information we have collected, sold, or shared in the past 12 months: as set forth in "What we collect" above. **We have not sold or shared personal information for cross-context behavioural advertising.**

To exercise your California rights, contact dpo@ordrtreasuryfx.com.
