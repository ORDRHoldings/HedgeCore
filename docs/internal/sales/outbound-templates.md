# ORDR TreasuryFX — Outbound Templates

**Purpose:** Five battle-tested outbound sequences for the corporate treasury buying committee. Cold email + LinkedIn + voicemail.

**Voice rules:**
- No "Hope this finds you well." Treasurers and CFOs read it as filler and skip.
- One specific claim per email. Never two.
- Always a low-friction CTA (one calendar click). Never "30 minutes" in the first email — say "15 minutes."
- Subject lines under 50 characters.
- No exclamation marks. No emojis.
- Sign with first name only. Brevity signals seniority.

- *"Touching base"* (filler)
- Anything with "synergy," "leverage," "optimize," "transform"

## Targeting filters (for outbound list building)

**Firmographic:**
- Revenue: $50M – $2B
- Industry: Manufacturing, SaaS (multi-currency MRR), e-commerce, pharma, prof services with cross-border consulting
- Geography: EU (EMIR), UK (post-Brexit), US (ASC 815), DACH (governance-heavy)

**Triggers:**
- Recent CFO or Treasurer hire (LinkedIn alert)
- Recent funding round (>$10M) — they're scaling treasury
- Recent acquisition — multi-entity netting newly relevant
- Recent IPO filing — hedge accounting becomes formalized
- Posted on LinkedIn about audit findings, EMIR, or IFRS 9
- Listed in EMIR public registry as an active reporter

**Job titles to target:**
- Group Treasurer / VP Treasury / Director, Treasury
- CFO / Group CFO / VP Finance
- Group Controller / VP Controller
- Head of Risk / Chief Risk Officer
- Head of Internal Audit
- Compliance Officer (for regulated industries)

**Avoid:**
- Anyone in a treasury team larger than 30 — they have Reval/Murex
- Anyone at a bank or broker-dealer — wrong customer
- Government / NGO — wrong customer

## Cadence rules

| Day | Action |
|---|---|
| 0 | Email 1 |
| 2 | LinkedIn connection request |
| 4 | Email 2 |
| 7 | LinkedIn message (if connected) |
| 11 | Email 3 (breakup) |
| 14 | Voicemail (only if title is C-suite or VP) |
| 90 | Re-touch only if a clear trigger fires |

Never more than 5 touches in 14 days. Never reuse a sequence within 6 months.

## What to track

For every prospect:
- Source (which list / which trigger)
- Sequence used
- Open + reply rate per email
- Meeting booked: yes/no
- Disposition: closed-won / closed-lost / nurture / disqualified
- Disqualification reason (use Kyriba already / too small / wrong industry / no budget cycle)

Run this in a Google Sheet for the first 100 prospects. Move to a CRM (HubSpot / Attio) once the volume justifies it.
