### Pricing FAQ

**Why no per-user pricing?**
> Treasury teams shouldn't be punished for involving their auditor or controller. Seat-count caps are generous; we don't haggle on whether a person is a "user."

**Is there a free tier?**
> No, but Starter is a 90-day no-commitment trial with full functionality on a single entity. If it doesn't pay for itself in your first audit cycle, walk away.

**Can I switch tiers mid-contract?**
> Upgrade at any time, prorated. Downgrade at renewal.

**What's the implementation timeline?**
> Starter: 1 week, self-serve. Professional: 90 days from kickoff to first live hedge. Enterprise: ~120 days with custom integrations.

**Do you charge for ERP connectors?**
> No. All five ERP connectors (QuickBooks, Xero, NetSuite, Sage, Dynamics 365) are included on Professional and Enterprise.

**What's the cancellation policy?**
> Starter: cancel any time, prorated refund. Professional / Enterprise: 60-day notice before renewal.

**Do you negotiate?**
> On Enterprise pricing for multi-year, multi-entity, or strategic deals — yes. On Starter and standard Professional — no, the prices are the prices.

### Section: Trust principles (3 cards)

**Determinism over optimization**
> Our hedge calculation engine has zero machine-learning components. Every output can be defended to a regulator. Every calculation can be reproduced byte-for-byte from the inputs and the policy version.

**Tamper-evidence over trust**
> Every calculation is signed. Every policy change is signed. Every approval is signed. All linked into a SHA-256 hash chain that's verified daily. We don't ask you to trust us — we make tampering detectable.

**Separation over speed**
> Maker/checker is enforced in code. The same user can't propose and approve. The pipeline (SANDBOX → STAGING → LEDGER) physically separates analysis from production.
